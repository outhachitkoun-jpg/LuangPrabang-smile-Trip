Best of Laos: 5 Day Escape

5 Days Luang Prabang to Vientiane Private Tour

Tour Overview

Discover the spiritual heart and natural wonders of Laos in comfortably paced 5 days. From the UNESCO World Heritage streets of Luang Prabang to the limestone karsts of Vang Vieng and the charming capital of Vientiane, this journey perfectly balances culture, adventure, and relaxation.

Daily Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival in Luang Prabang

Sabaidee! Welcome to Luang Prabang. Your guide will meet you at the airport and transfer you to your boutique hotel. In the evening, witness a Baci ceremony and explore the vibrant Night Market.

  • Airport Transfer
  • Hotel Check-in
  • Night Market Visit
Day 2

Alms Giving & City Heritage

Rise early for the sacred Tak Bat (Alms Giving) ceremony. Spend the day exploring Wat Xieng Thong, the Royal Palace, and sunset atop Mount Phousi.

  • Alms Giving Ceremony
  • Guided City Tour
  • Mount Phousi Sunset
Day 3

Kuang Si Falls & Train to Vang Vieng

Swim in the turquoise pools of Kuang Si Waterfalls. In the afternoon, board the plush high-speed train to Vang Vieng and check into your riverside resort.

  • Kuang Si Waterfalls
  • Bear Rescue Center
  • Train to Vang Vieng
Day 4

Vang Vieng Adventure & Transfer to Vientiane

Morning kayaking on the Nam Song River or opt for a hot air balloon ride. Afternoon transfer to Vientiane, the relaxed capital city.

  • River Kayaking
  • Blue Lagoon
  • Transfer to Vientiane
Day 5

Vientiane Highlights & Departure

Visit the golden Pha That Luang stupa and Patuxai Monument before your airport transfer. Safe travels!

  • Pha That Luang
  • Patuxai Monument
  • Airport Transfer
