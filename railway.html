A Revolutionary Connection

The Laos-China Railway (LCR) is a landmark infrastructure project that has transformed travel in Laos. Stretching 422km from Vientiane to the border at Boten, it connects major tourist hubs and extends all the way to Kunming, China. What used to be a 10-hour drive is now a comfortable 2-hour journey.

Major Stations

Vientiane (VTE)

The southern terminus and capital station. Modern, massive, and very busy.

Vang Vieng (VVI)

Gateway to adventure and limestone karsts. Located just outside the town center.

Luang Prabang (LPQ)

The spiritual heart of Laos. Station is about 20 mins from the Old Town.

Muang Xay (MXA)

Gateway to Northern Laos and Oudomxay province.

Boten (BOT)

The border station for cross-border travel to China.
